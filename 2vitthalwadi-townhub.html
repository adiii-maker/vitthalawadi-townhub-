About the town

Small station, big neighbourhood.

Vithalwadi sits right on the Central Railway line, sandwiched between Kalyan and Ulhasnagar, in Kalyan East. The locality grew up around its namesake Vithoba temple and the daily rhythm of the Vithalwadi Railway Station — one of the busiest small halts on the Mumbai suburban network. It's a dense, lively mix of chawls, housing societies, family-run shops and the everyday services that keep a commuter town running.

Most visitors use Vithalwadi as a base to explore the wider Kalyan East–Kalyan West area: the historic Durgadi Fort, the redeveloped Kala Talao lake, the riverside Ganesh Ghat, and the hill town of Malanggad further out. This page collects the practical stuff — food, shopping, hospitals and sights — in one place.

Sights worth a visit

Tourist places near Vithalwadi

Vithalwadi itself is a quiet residential stretch, but it sits within easy reach of Kalyan's best-known landmarks — forts, lakes, temples and a riverside ghat. Best season: October to March.

Vithoba Temple
Temple · In Vithalwadi

Vithoba Temple

The neighbourhood's namesake shrine, dedicated to Vithoba (Vitthal). A modest, well-loved temple that anchors local festivals and daily prayer life in Vithalwadi.

Durgadi Fort
Fort · ~3 km

Durgadi Fort

A small fort on the Kalyan creek with a Kali temple inside, linked to the Maratha era. Compact and easy to walk around, with pleasant river views — best visited in the early morning or evening.

Kala Talao
Lake & Garden · ~3 km

Kala Talao

One of the oldest community lakes in Kalyan, now redeveloped with a walking promenade, boating, a small garden and evening light-and-water shows. Popular for morning walks and family evenings out.

Ganesh Ghat
Riverfront · ~3 km

Ganesh Ghat

Locally called the "Chowpatty" of Kalyan — stone steps lead down to the Ulhas river. Lively at sunset, and at its busiest during Ganpati visarjan season.

Birla Mandir
Temple · Ulhasnagar

Birla Mandir, Ulhasnagar

A well-known temple near the Century Rayon company area, also recognised as a filming location for several Hindi films over the decades.

Malanggad
Hill shrine / trek · day trip

Haji Malang (Malanggad)

A hilltop dargah revered by both Hindu and Muslim devotees, reached via a trek or ropeway from the base village. A popular day-trip destination from the Kalyan area.

Metro Junction Mall
Modern attraction · Kalyan West

Metro Junction Mall

Kalyan's go-to modern hangout — shopping, a food court and a multiplex, useful when you want air-conditioned comfort after a day of sightseeing.

Kondana Caves
Ancient caves · day trip

Kondana Caves

Government-maintained Buddhist rock-cut caves carved into a hillside, well outside the city bustle. A good spot for history lovers and a quiet walk.

Ulhas Riverside
Riverside · In Vithalwadi

Ulhas River Bend, Vithalwadi

The stretch of the Ulhas river that runs past Vithalwadi itself — a calmer, more local counterpart to Ganesh Ghat, good for an early-morning walk.
